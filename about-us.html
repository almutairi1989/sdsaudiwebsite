About Us

Advancing healthcare in Saudi Arabia since 2012

Specialized Distributor Est. is a Saudi Arabian provider of cutting-edge medical device and technology solutions, with a particular focus on Radiology and Interventional Radiology.

Our Story

Who we are

Specialized Distributor Est., established in 2012, partners with global innovators to deliver a comprehensive range of high-quality, cost-effective solutions that empower healthcare professionals to achieve optimal patient outcomes.

Our expertise extends beyond product distribution to include medical solutions and radiology-related IT services.

Our Mission

To consistently provide value to our customers by offering safe, patient-centric, and innovative medical equipment, devices, and IT solutions — contributing to the advancement of healthcare in Saudi Arabia.

Our Vision

To be the premier provider of world-class medical supplies solutions, empowering both healthcare professionals and patients.
